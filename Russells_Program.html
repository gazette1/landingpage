Reference
Extras & Programming
Core-Specific Session (2x/week)

Perform on off days (Mon, Wed, Sun). Circuit format, 3-4 rounds.

Circuit (60-90s rest between rounds)
  • Plank: 60 seconds
  • Side plank: 45 sec each side
  • Russian twists w/ KB: 20 reps
  • Cable Pallof press: 12 reps each side
  • Dead bug: 12 reps per side
  • Cable wood chops: 15 reps each side
  • Bicycle crunches: 20 reps
Cardio Sessions (2x/week)

Perform on off days or after training sessions.

Choose one per session
  • Steady-State: 25-30 min, 6-7/10 effort
  • HIIT: 30s hard / 90s recovery × 8-10 rounds
  • Incline Walk: 8-12% incline, 3.0-3.5 mph, 25-30 min
Progressive Overload
WeekProtocol
1-2Learn movements, moderate weight, form focus
3-4Add 5-10 lbs to main lifts, maintain reps
5-6Add 1-2 reps per set or reduce rest 15s
7-8Increase weight again or add 1 set to mains
9DELOAD: reduce weight 30%, maintain volume
10+Restart cycle with higher baseline
Intensity Guide
RPE Targets
  • Main lifts (6-10 reps): Leave 1-2 reps in reserve
  • Accessory (12-15 reps): Can approach near failure
  • Power movements: Speed and quality over weight
  • Rotational: Control and speed equally important

Injury Prevention
Non-Negotiables
  • Never skip warm-ups
  • Progress weight gradually (5-10 lbs/week max)
  • Pain ≠ fatigue. If it hurts, stop.
  • Pit Shark + KBs only for legs. No spinal loading.
  • Belt low across hips on Pit Shark
Recovery & Nutrition
Recovery
  • Sleep: 7-9 hours nightly
  • Active recovery: light walking or swimming on off days
  • Foam rolling: 10 min pre and post workout
  • Stretching: 10-15 min daily (hips + thoracic spine)
Nutrition
  • Protein: 0.8-1.0g per lb bodyweight daily
  • Carbs: pre-workout meal 1-2 hours before
  • Water: 64-80+ oz daily, more on training days
  • Post-workout: protein shake or meal within 60 min
