About this prototype

  
Rule-based and fully client-side; nothing on this page is transmitted anywhere. It demonstrates the intake-to-downstream pattern: extraction with per-field confidence, policy screening, human-in-the-loop exceptions, and an attributable audit trail. The production version is an agentic extraction pipeline with an evaluation harness, model-confidence routing, and posting into the systems of record.

  
    
    
  • Extraction with evals, not vibesLLM extraction scored against a labeled regression set per document type; confidence thresholds tuned to route uncertainty to humans, and every model change re-scored before deploy.
    • 
    
  • Human-in-the-loop by designApprovers see the finding, the evidence, and the suggested action in one place; decisions feed back into reusable rule and prompt patterns.
    • 
    
  • Enterprise fitDeploys inside the firm's environment with observability, access controls, and a full audit trail; posts to the ERP and approval systems of record.
